How has the coronavirus affected you?

This post would hit you hard if you are still away from home. Yes, because I am.

World health organisation(WHO) has said that like HIV, the novel coronavirus could become endemic and may never go away. This sounds horrible right? Yes. That means, there would be larger impact on lives. We have lived quarantine nearly 3 month and It has been affected our everyday life and quite possibilities that we have to cope with it in long term. Some bullets shows my life in quarantine.

  1. I don't know how my gym looks like.

  2. Pillow and books are my friends now.

  3. My friends are with their parents. Since I am still away from my home town so, there is crave for seeing my parents in person.

  4. We are three in a room, hate cooking, still manage to cook two time a day.

  5. We have shortage of things. Daliya, khichadi are life saviour for us.

  6. Quarantine time gives me to learn guitar lessons.

  7. Since there is no professional life. So,we becomes owl of our own city.

  8. Spending time with friends in person becomes dream nowadays.

  9. I will have to wait more to join corporate world; first salary.

  10. There is many more. Somehow, we have to deal with everything.
