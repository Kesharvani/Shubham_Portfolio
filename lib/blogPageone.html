As a teenager, what bothers you most about society today?

Discrimination faced by the girl child bothers me the most. It is tough to digest if it starts with your father.

Let me tell you a story.

Shital Hound was in her 20’s, born in a wealthy family, never found love from her parent.

Her father, Sam Hound, barely talked to her because he believed that the only son could hold his huge empire and girls are useless.

After she was born, her mother died at the hospital.

She became the president of Hound & sons after the early death of his father.

Hound& son were in debt and bankers refuse to give loan to the company because the president of the company was a lady.

Bankers believed that the company couldn’t run by a woman and it would be a waste of their money. But they were agreed to give load if the president would be a man.

Shital had faced discrimination not only by society but by her family too.

Shital had always regretted that why she came into this world. Even she didn't do anything wrong, just because she was a girl child, everyone was punishing her.

The girls should be treated the same as you treat your son.

Father has the family's reins. So, it totally depends upon father that which type of society the father wants.



This story is just an example. I have seen in my circumstances that how people differentiate on the basis of gender. They always give importance to the boy.

Treat equally. This would lead to a better society.
Don't discriminate.
